And sometimes you act like a child which is really really really cute and I love it. Out of all the letters this was by far the easiest to write, because there are so many things I could say about why I love you. I love you more than I’ve ever loved anyone in my life. I think I even love you more than I love my brothers. I definitely love you as much as I love them, probably more. I hope you love me as much as I love you. I feel like you don’t but that’s okay.
